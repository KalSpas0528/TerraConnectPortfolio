Background - What is TerraConnect?

TerraConnect is an initiative designed to bridge the gap between environmental research and technology. It aims to develop AI-powered tools for data analysis, resource tracking, and sustainability efforts.

Week 1 - LinkedIn, Resume, Portfolio

  • Optimize LinkedIn profile with a focus on technology and environmental projects.
  • Update resume with relevant skills and past experience.
  • Develop an online portfolio showcasing past projects and research interests.

Week 2 - Research Planning Document

  • Define the research scope for TerraConnect.
  • Gather existing literature and case studies.
  • Identify key challenges and potential AI applications.

Week 3 - Project Scoping & Initial Setup

  • Define problem statement and research background.
  • Research AI integration possibilities.
  • Outline preliminary task list.
  • Set up GitHub repository and documentation structure.
  • Identify key stakeholders and potential collaborators.

Week 4 - Development, Mentorship, & Portfolio Enhancement

  • Clarify mentor expectations and guidance needs.
  • Organize research materials and resources.
  • Begin coding with regular GitHub commits.
  • Update portfolio with project description and clickable timeline.
  • Refine project goals based on early feedback.
