Background - What is TerraConnect?

TerraConnect is an initiative designed to bridge the gap between environmental research and technology. It aims to develop AI-powered tools for data analysis, resource tracking, and sustainability efforts.

Concept & Research (Weeks 1-2)

  • Optimize LinkedIn and update resume to highlight AI and sustainability focus.
  • Develop online portfolio showcasing past projects and research interests.
  • Define the research scope for TerraConnect.
  • Gather existing literature and identify AI applications.

Scoping & Setup (Weeks 3-4)

  • Define problem statement and research background.
  • Research AI integration possibilities and system architecture.
  • Set up GitHub repository and documentation structure.
  • Identify key stakeholders and potential collaborators.

Development & Integration (Weeks 5-7)

  • Clarify mentor expectations and guidance needs.
  • Begin coding with regular GitHub commits.
  • Develop AI models and integrate environmental datasets.
  • Refine project goals based on early feedback.

Testing & Refinement (Weeks 8-9)

  • Perform iterative testing of AI models and optimize performance.
  • Gather user feedback and make improvements.
  • Update portfolio with project description and findings.

Finalization & Deployment (Weeks 10-12)

  • Finalize AI model and system architecture.
  • Deploy the project for real-world application.
  • Present findings and prepare for future iterations.
